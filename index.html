Grade-Wise Learning Path

Our CBSE-aligned robotics curriculum is designed to nurture logical thinking, creativity, and real-world problem-solving skills through hands-on learning.

Primary Level: Introduction to robotics, logic building, and visual programming.

Middle School: Sensors, motors, automation concepts, and structured coding.

Senior Grades: AI fundamentals, IoT, real-world robotics applications, and innovation projects.

Designed for CBSE schools to seamlessly integrate robotics and AI into academic learning while meeting national education objectives.
